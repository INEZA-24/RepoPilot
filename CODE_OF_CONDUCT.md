# Code of Conduct

RepoPilot is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and productive open source community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect differing experience levels.
- Help contributors learn how to participate effectively.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or hostile review comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Disruptive behavior that prevents collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior does not meet this code. Reports can be sent to the project maintainers through the repository's public contact channels.
